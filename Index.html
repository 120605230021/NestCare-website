Our Services

We offer comprehensive care packages that combine genuine compassion with modern technology to ensure the health and happiness of seniors.

Booking Hospital Appointments & Queue Service

NestCare's booking service allows families to schedule an appointment, eliminating the need to wait in long queues in hospitals. On our platform, households can select a suitable time range, register their parents' statistics and get confirmation. Thanks to this, the elderly just need to show up in time to see the doctor.

Health Tracking App

Our app gives family members a real-time health index of their parents, regardless of the distance between them. Through the app, families can display key health indicators like blood pressure and heart rate and update health information results from the hospital. The app offers fall detection (only works with the list of watches we provide), which we can get families reserved for immediate indicators if elderly parents fall down.
